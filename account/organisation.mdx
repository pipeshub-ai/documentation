### Overview

The application supports two types of organization account types:

- Individual Account
- Enterprise Account

Each account type has distinct features and permissions to cater to different user needs.

## Enterprise Account

### User Invitation Process

1. Navigate to Users & Groups under Company Settings. Click on Users Tabs and then on Invite Users.
2. Enter one or multiple email addresses.
3. Select one or more groups for the new users.
4. Send invitations. Users will receive an email with login details.

## Common Features

### Editing Organisation Details

**1. Overview**

Users can update their **organization details** and **personal information** directly within the application. This allows for better account management and ensures that profile information remains up to date.

**2. Editing Organization Details**

Admins can modify key details related to their organization, including:

- **Organization Name**
- **Business Type**
- **Industry**
- **Company Address**
- **Contact Information**
- **Logo Upload**

### Steps to Edit Organization Details

1. Navigate to **Company Profile**.
2. Modify the required fields.
3. Click **Save Changes** to update the information.

### Editing Personal Details

All users can modify their own profile details, including:

- **Full Name**
- **Profile Picture**
- **Designation**
- **Email Address**
- **Password**

### Steps to Edit Personal Details

1. Go to **Profile Settings** .
2. Update the necessary fields.
3. Click **Save Changes** to apply the updates.

## Conclusion

Editing organization and personal details ensures that user profiles and company information remain up to date. Admins can manage company settings, while users have control over their personal data within the platform.
